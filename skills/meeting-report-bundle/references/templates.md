# Templates

## `report.md` template

```md
# <Meeting Title>

## Metadata

- Date:
- Duration:
- Attendees:
- Meeting type:
- Audience:
- Detail level:

## Executive Summary

- 3-6 bullets

## Decisions Made

- Decision:
  - Rationale:
  - Owner:
  - Impact:

## Risks and Blockers

- Risk/blocker:
  - Severity:
  - Mitigation:
  - Owner:

## Action Items

- [ ] Action - Owner - Due date - Status

## Open Questions

- Question - Owner - Next checkpoint

## Notes / Evidence

- Key notes, context, and references
```

## `audio-script.txt` template

```txt
Here is your meeting follow-up.

Top decisions: <1-3 concise points>.
Main risks or blockers: <1-3 concise points>.
Immediate next actions with owners: <concise owner-bound list>.

If escalation is required, call out who needs to act and by when.
```
